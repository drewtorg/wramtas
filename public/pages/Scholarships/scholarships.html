Four scholarships will be awarded to assist with the cost of attending WR/MWRAMTA Co-Conference in Broomfield, CO in March.

Students applying for scholarships must meet the following requirements:

  • AMTA Student Member: at the time of submission AND at the time of regional conference (March 13, 2017).
  • Currently enrolled in an AMTA approved university music therapy program AMTA affiliated internship within the Western Region

2017 Scholarships:

Songwriting Scholarship:

Two (2) $100 WRAMTAS Song Writing Scholarships will be awarded to assist with the cost of attending WR/MWRAMTA Co-Conference in Broomfield, CO in March.

Students applying for the Songwriting Scholarship must submit an original song that highlights the benefits of being an AMTA member. Please include the following:

  • mp3 recording of original song
  • Lyric sheet of original song

Advocacy Essay Scholarship:

Two (2) $100 WRAMTAS Advocacy Essay Scholarships will be awarded to assist with the cost of attending WR/MWRAMTA Co-Conference in Broomfield, CO in March.

Students applying for the Advocacy Essay Scholarship must submit two essays with the following prompts:

  • Essay One: (700-1,000 words) How do you advocate for music therapy at home and in the community?
  • Essay Two: (150 words max) a physician at a medical center stops you in the hallway and asks, “What is music therapy?” What is your response?
